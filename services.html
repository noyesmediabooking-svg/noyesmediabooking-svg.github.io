HDR Photography
Service 01

Real Estate
Photography

Every image is individually edited for colour accuracy, white balance, lens distortion correction, and sky replacement when needed. The final result: a gallery that looks polished and true to life.

Our Approach

How Every
Shoot Works

A streamlined process designed around your schedule and your listing timeline. No complexity, no chasing — just results delivered fast.

01

Book & Brief

Select your services and submit your booking through our contact form. We'll confirm availability, discuss any specific features of the property you want highlighted, and lock in a shoot time that works around showings and sellers.

02

Pre-Shoot Prep Guidance

We send a simple preparation checklist to the listing agent or seller — what to declutter, how to set lighting, which exterior areas to tidy. A well-prepared property always photographs better, and we help set you up for that.

03

On-Site Shoot

We arrive on time, work efficiently, and treat the property with care. We move through each room systematically — photography, drone (if included), floor plan measurements, and video in a single visit whenever possible to minimize disruption.

04

Edit & Deliver

Post-processing begins same-day. Standard delivery is within 24 hours. Rush 6-hour delivery available as an add-on. You'll receive a private online gallery with all files organized, labelled, and ready to download in every format you need.

05

Revisions & Support

Not satisfied with a shot? We'll fix it. Premium package clients get unlimited revisions. All packages include one round of edit revisions. We're not done until you're confident your listing is presented at its absolute best.
